What is this really?

To celebrate the coming of a new era we have teamed up with disneyland to hold a annual clean up theme park. We are happy to announce that this giveaway is free of charge all you need to do is sign up so get on your bike and get ready to make the world a better place.

How to enter

Its as simple as riding a bike!. All you've got to do is take a picture with your friends showing an act of kindness and gratitude that you performed to make the world a better place. You can either Reduce, Reuse or Recycle or just even ride a flipping bike . Our team over here will decide the winners next month and remember everyone is a winner here since we are all helping the environment.
